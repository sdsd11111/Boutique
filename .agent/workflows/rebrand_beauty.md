# Rebranding Workflow: Lumina Beauty & Wellness

Follow these steps to transform the existing light warehouse landing page into a premium beauty salon.

## 1. Update Design System
- Open `src/app/globals.css`
- Replace current colors with:
  - `--background`: #FDF4F5 (Soft Rose White)
  - `--foreground`: #2D2727 (Deep Charcoal)
  - `--premium-gold`: #D4AF37 (Luxury Gold)
  - `--accent-rose`: #E8A0BF (Dusty Rose)
- Ensure typography uses `Playfair Display` for headers and `Inter` for body.

## 2. Update Content
- **Header**: Change "NALUZ" to "LUMINA" and "Asesoría Directa" to "Saca tu Cita".
- **Hero**:
  - Title: "Renueva tu Belleza, Resalta tu Luz."
  - Description: "Experiencia premium en cuidado personal y bienestar en el corazón de Loja."
- **About Us**: Focus on the experience, tranquility, and natural essence.
- **Services Gallery**: Replace electrical products with:
  - Limpieza Facial Profunda
  - Estilismo Profesional
  - Manicure & Pedicure Premium
  - Tratamientos Corporales

## 3. Replace Assets
- Use the generated images for Hero and Services.
- Ensure all images have appropriate alt tags reflecting beauty services.

## 4. Final Review
- Check mobile responsiveness.
- Verify all links (especially WhatsApp) are correct for the new context.
